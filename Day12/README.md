One situation where VPN is useful as per my understanding :
A VPN is useful when connecting to public Wi-Fi networks such as in cafes, airports, or hotels. It encrypts the internet traffic and protects sensitive data from attackers who might be monitoring the network.

One thing VPN does NOT hide :
A VPN does not hide your identity if you are logged into online accounts like Google, social media platforms, or other services.

The one wrong belief I had about VPNs:
Earlier I believed that VPN makes a user completely anonymous on the internet. However, I learned that websites can still track users using cookies, browser fingerprinting, or account logins.
